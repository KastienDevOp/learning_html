ButtlerBot Jobs -- Mayor Information

Being The Mayor Of The City

First and foremost the mayor of the city is the owner of the discord server. There is no way that I would be able to setup an entire election process that would allow members to elect who the mayor of the city would be and it allow the actual guild owner to remain in charge as the discord server in its entirety is virtually converted into a city by inviting ButtlerBot to the server. With that being said, the Mayors job is to review reports that are generated from the owner of each company and sent in so that the mayor can see whether the pay rates set for each job is sufficient enough to be maintained by the initial starting guild balance ($1,000,000,000). The Mayor also retains the ability to remove any company owner from power given that corruption has taken over and the employees have gone on strike. In order for employees to go on strike, more than 60% of the companies employees must use the !strike command and place themselves on strike. When the employees go on strike, they lose all pay and benefits. In the event that the company is forced to close and a new owner takes over, the 401K that has been paid in for each employee per pay day is therein left at the same amount, each employees pay increases by $100 and their prestige level increases by 1.

Upon Inviting ButtlerBot To Your Server!

When you invite Buttlerbot to your server, please be aware that the bot is going to ask you a series of questions. I know this sounds like a pain in the butt, but it's so that each mayor can setup their city in the mannor that they choose. As a person myself, I do not have the liberty of telling any guild owner what they can or cannot do with their server but I am able to provide extra options that my bot will automatically handle for them.

  • Things that will happen when ButtlerBot Joins Your Server:
    • Question 1:
      • Do You Want To Participate In ButtlerBot City Extension?
        • If the guild owner selects yes, then the bot will create all needed roles, database keys/values, text_channels and their perspective categories, and currencies needed to create the city within the server.
        • If the guild owner selects no, then the bot will not create anything except the basic items needed to have the moderation and casino games (other basic extensions as well)
