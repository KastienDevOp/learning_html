ButtlerBot Information

ButtlerBot will soon be the bot for all bots. What I mean is, Buttlerbot will be able to do nearly everything for you from moderating your guild. From ensuring that your cities have enough money and resources to survive to filtering out unwanted words within the communities chat channels. Buttlerbot is being built for much much more, though. In the years to come, we plan to setup ButtlerBot Discord Vs 3.0 to where one discord server (guild henceforth) will be able to have multiple cities to allow for dictatorships, and other types of lifestyles that each city may want. Also incorporated into ButtlerBot is a suggestion command. I highly encourage you to use this command as it will send your suggestions straight to my database for me to be able to view. I have a taskloop setup that pulls the first 20 suggestions per week in the database for me to view. Upon approval, the extension will be created and once ready for action, a notice will be put out in the newsletter by Buttlerbot that shows all types of awesome information! So don't forget to subscribe to the newsletter !subnews so that every Monday you get the newsletter right in your DM's!
